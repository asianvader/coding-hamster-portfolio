About Me

My career has taken many twists and turns and I didn’t have a conventional start into web development. I worked in the Higher Education sector for over a decade before becoming a Frontend Web Developer.

I’m self-taught, following various online learning web platforms such as freeCodeCamp, Codecademy and Lynda.com.

I am now freelancing as a Frontend Web Developer. I can design and build bespoke responsive websites from scratch or customise website templates. My current interests are in JavaScript and frameworks/libraries such as Bootstrap, React and Redux.
