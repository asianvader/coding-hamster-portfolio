About Me

My career as a Frontend Web Developer began recently after a few years of dabbling in web development as a hobby. Before that I had a career in Higher Education and worked at some prestigious universities in London. I have a passion for education and enjoying my ongoing learning experience into all the latest web technologies.

Not a conventional start...

After graduating with a History degree from the London School of Economics and Political Science, I continued my career where my education had stopped. I went into university administration at King’s College London and moved to Imperial College London. I soon realised my passion was creating solutions for cumbersome administrative processes. I was an advocate for automation where possible to free up valuable time to do something more creative and productive. I progressed to project managing bespoke IT projects.

I moved out of London after the birth of youngest baby and decided to pursue a different career path into web development. I’m completely self-taught, following various online learning web platforms such as Freecodecamp, Codecademy and Lynda.com. I have completed two certifications from Freecodecamp so far: Responsive Web Design Certification and Javascript Algorithms and Data Structures Certification.

I am continuing with my studies and now freelancing as a Frontend Web Developer. My current interests are in vanilla Javascript and frameworks/libraries such as Bootstrap, React and Redux.
